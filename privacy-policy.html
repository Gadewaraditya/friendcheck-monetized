Servers Storing Your Data

FriendCheck is a static entertainment website. Everything runs in your browser. We have no backend servers, no user databases, and no login system.

📊 Information We Collect

We collect very minimal information:

  • Names you type — entered by you into tools (e.g., friendship tests, roast generator). These are processed entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to us.
  • LocalStorage data — some tools (like the Prank Level system) store progress data locally in your browser's localStorage. This data lives only on your device and is never sent anywhere.
  • Basic analytics — we may use standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics or Cloudflare analytics) that collect anonymised page view data, browser type, and general location (country-level). No personally identifiable information is collected.
🚫 What We Do NOT Collect
  • Your real name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact us voluntarily)
  • Any payment or financial information (we are completely free)
  • Your social media accounts or friend lists
  • Precise location data or device identifiers
  • Any data from minors — this site is intended for users 13 and above
🍪 Cookies

We may use cookies for basic site functionality and analytics. These cookies do not contain personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting the core functionality of our tools.

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Some pages load resources from third-party CDNs (Google Fonts, cdnjs.cloudflare.com) for fonts and libraries. These services may set their own cookies per their respective privacy policies. We do not control these services.

If you use the contact form or email us directly, your email provider's privacy policy also applies.

👶 Children's Privacy

FriendCheck is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of FriendCheck after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
